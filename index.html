I am an traditionally trained allopathic medical doctor licensed by the Arizona Board of Homeopathic and Integrated Medicine Examiners. While I can and do prescribe traditional medications, I focus on the whole person utilizing a whole health approach to prevention, health, and well-being employing a biological framework to find and treat the root causes of a disease or disorder creating a personalized patient care plan and an individualized medical experience. This means I may recommend dietary changes and supplements along with or instead of medications.

The functional medicine model of care offers a patient-centered approach to chronic disease management. My approach seeks to answer the question, “Why are you ill?”, not just "take this pill".

You receive personalized and effective care. My philosophy of medical care uses a frame work for understanding wholeness and health, utilizing a combination of conventional medication, nutrition, supplements, along with education and personalized counseling.
